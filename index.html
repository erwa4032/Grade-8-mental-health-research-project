Oppositional Defiant Disorder(ODD)

By: Eric Wan 8A


What is ODD?

The oppositional defiant disorder is a disorder that make people behave innaproprietly

It mostly happens in childrens. This disease causes them to be uncooperative with parents, teachers and any other people.



How is it caused or how does it manifest?

It is caused by biological, phsycological, or social factors. That means the surrounding environment plays a very important role. However, the exact cause of this disorder is still unknown.



What are the effects?

It has a lot of bad effects. For example a well behaved children can get into big trouble after getting ODD. People who have ODD can blame others for their own faults, complain about rules, being mean, seeking revenge, or simply annoying teachers or parents



Where can ODD be found, how common is it?

About 2-16 percent had ODD. It can occur as young as 8 years old. It can be expected to occur anywhere in the world



What to do to fix ODD?

1. Talking with parents.
2. Problem solving
3. Interacting
4. Talking positively



how to get support?

1. Children with good education from parents can also have ODD.
2. Study shows that punishing ODD behaviour will not help.
3. Usually ODD goes away before 3 years.


how to get support?

1. Parents
2. Teachers
3. Psychiatrist or doctors.
